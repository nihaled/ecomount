LEGACY

A scientist in every act, word, and deed, Dr. Abdul Salam sees matters from a different perspective. He gives a new dimension to the mundane and quickly identifies opportunities in what comes in front of him. He has earned his academic credentials from prestigious institutions in India and abroad, and has spent over three decades leading groundbreaking initiatives that have transformed the future. In a career spanning more than three decades, after completing B. Tech from NIT, M. Tech from Aligarh Muslim University, and a Ph.D. from the Malaysian Institute of Technology, he chose to become an advisor, scientist, strategic consultant, and head of departments on many development projects across India and abroad. He was the executive advisor for AL Munawwara development projects, as well as a consultant for the Abu Dhabi government's property management sector and the Al Mukarrama Land Management project in Saudi Arabia. He also worked as the head of planning and projects for the public works departments in Qatar as well as an officer in the Planning Commission's National Informatics Center, besides his stint at ISRO in the role of a scientist. He came back to India to create the first-of-its-kind Markaz Knowledge City with cutting- edge education, technology, and healthcare institutions surrounded by a group of other companies that complete the perfect new generation city. At Ecomount, he drives the sustainable vision of the brand through scientific interventions. His passion for science and strategic thinking has made him a valuable asset to every project he has undertaken, cementing his reputation as an exceptional leader and innovator.
